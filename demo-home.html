Nelson Gentry's dedication to the Berkeley District is driven by personal experience and deep roots.

I've lived in Spotsylvania County for over 50 years, and I've also worked for Spotsylvania County for over 31 years. I am a proud husband, father and resident. I own two small businesses. I currently farm over 275 acres across Spotsylvania County. Throughout my 31 year career in Spotsylvania County, I have taken pride in solving difficult problems big and small. Planning and solving problems for a living, I know the value of being a responsive, strong leader. When elected to the Board of Supervisors, I will be just as engaged by demanding fiscal accountability by all departments, leading the fight of smart growth.

We will fight together for fiscal responsibility and common sense in Spotsylvania County.

I would be honored to have your support.
